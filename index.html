Assignment 2 of the Module GEOG5991M

Final Project. White Star Line

The purpose of the Web Page is to describe the requirements of the Assignment 2 of the Module GEOG5991M and provide the links.

White Star Line Project is suggested to create the process of identification of the icebergs from the Lidar and Radar images.
Briefly, the goal is to analyse the radar and lidar images of the sea and identify if the bergs can be pulled by tug or not. In addition to the requirements to assess tow-ability of the icebergs by colours, the interaction with the result image (see Figure 1) was enabled to view the analysed characteristics of the bergs.
